Banana Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • Chilled Banana
  • Ice
  • Milk of choice
  • Any yogurt(optional)
  • Seeds(optional)
  • Protein Powerder(optional)

Instructions:

1. Cut the chilled bananas.
2. Blend the chilled bananas.
3. Add the milk and ice.
4. Add the seeds and protein powder.
5. Garnish and server.

Omelet

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Oil
  • Chives or Green Onions(optional)

Instructions:

1. Prepare the pan until hot.
2. Whisk the egg until combined.
3. Add seasonings.
4. Place the whisked eggs on the pan.
5. Wait for 3-5 mins.
6. Garnish then serve.

Baked Potatoes

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Oil

Instructions:

1. Preheat oven to 400F(200C).
2. Wash and poke holes in the potato with fork.
3. Bake for 45-60 minutes until soft.
4. Slice open and add butter or salt and pepper or all combined if you want.
5. Plate the baked potatoes and serve.
