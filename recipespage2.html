Avocado Toast

Ingredients:

  • Avocado
  • 2 slices of bread
  • Butter
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. Toast your bread.
2. Mash the avocadoes.
3. Add your seasonings.
4. Place the avocados over the toast and serve.

Boiled Eggs

Ingredients:

  • Water
  • Eggs

Instructions:

1. Boil the water.
2. Place the eggs in the boiling water.
3. Wait for 7-10 mins.
4. Cool the eggs down.
5. Peel then server.

Fruit Salad

Ingredients:

  • Fruits of choice
  • Milk
  • Sweetener of choice
  • Ice(optional)

Instructions:

1. Cut the fruits.
2. Place fruits in clean bowl.
3. Add the milk and sweetener of choice.
4. Mix well and serve.

Insant Ramen

Ingredients:

  • Water
  • Instant ramen noodles
  • Egg/s(optional)

Instructions:

1. Boil the water.
2. Place the instant noodles and eggs in the boiling water.
3. Wait for 7-10 mins.
4. Serve.
