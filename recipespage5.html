Baked Sweet Potato

Ingredients:

  • Honey
  • Greek Yogurt
  • Wallnuts(optional)

Instructions:

1. Prepare both the honey and the greek yogurt.
2. Place both in a clean bowl.
3. Mix well and serve.

Banana Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 Banana
  • Eggs
  • Sugar to taste

Instructions:

1. Mash banana and mix with the egg.
2. Pour small amounts into a pan like pancakes.
3. Plate then serve.
