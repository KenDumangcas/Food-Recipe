Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. Whisk eggs in a bowl.
2. Melt butter in a pan and add eggs.
3. Stir continuously until cooked.
4. Season and serve.

Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • 1 tbsp butter

Instructions:

1. Butter the bread and place cheese in between.
2. Cook in a pan until golden brown.
3. Serve warm and enjoy.

Simple Toast

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Butter or jam

Instructions:

1. Toast the bread in a toaster or on a pan.
2. Spread butter or jam while warm.
3. Serve immediately.

Quick Oatmeal

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 1 cup milk or water
  • 1 tbsp honey

Instructions:

1. Boil milk or water.
2. Add oats and cook for 5 minutes.
3. Stir, add honey, and serve.
